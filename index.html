About

Researcher and engineer building AI-driven communication and autonomous systems.

I am an M.Sc. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Manitoba. My work focuses on wireless communication, machine learning, reinforcement learning, physical-layer modeling, and simulation-based optimization for future wireless systems.

Alongside research, I have worked on UAV autonomy, object detection, edge AI deployment, embedded hardware projects, FPGA design, networking analysis, and robotics/control simulations.

Graduate Research Assistant

Wireless Communication, Networking and Services Lab · University of Manitoba

Sep 2024 to Present

Winnipeg, Canada
